# <div style="text-align: center; color: black;">Countinuos integration</div> 

## Context:

#### The project management and development team was impressed by the effectiveness and efficiency of the automated and containerized testing solutions. Improvements include reduced resource consumption, as well as greater scalability and a shorter time to market for deployments.
#### As a result, the changes were implemented in a chaotic manner. The operations team has to perform very repetitive tasks every time the developers make a change. 

#### The development and operations team attended an event where they learned about the culture and concepts of continuous integration, continuous delivery, and continuous deployment. The employees realized that their challenges could be resolved by adopting this culture.
